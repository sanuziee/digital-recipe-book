Classic Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 300g spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  3. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions, drain, and serve with the Bolognese sauce. Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
