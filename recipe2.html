Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons (juice and zest)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate chicken breasts in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  5. Serve with your favorite sides.
